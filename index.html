About Us

TechTrack

TechTrack is a mobile application developed by aspiring group students from FEU Institute of Technology named ITech.

ITech is a Freelance company composed of aspiring students coming from FEU Institute of Technology who mainly focuses on creating, innovating, and solving social issues that might help the community for implementing and making their ideas into work. Through our knowledge on our specific field (web, mobile, system) applications we are able to fulfill the market needs and concerns.

Our company still relies on being a free-lance. It is not incorporated with an entity or does not hold shares, assets, stocks. ITech is a group of aspiring students who mainly comprises web, mobile, systems and software developers based in Metro-Manila Philippines with an indefinite working space. Since we are a group freelancer we rarely do meet in person; usually we tend to do meet ups online to discuss anything related to the company such as the development of the application, the business side and the financial aspect of our company. We are also trying to increase our numbers to further divide and make the work easier for us to do.
